FUTA Rafting

Experience the thrill of white water rafting in one of the world's most breathtaking rivers. At FUTA Rafting, we turn nature's power into unforgettable adventures—guided by locals, rooted in tradition, and fueled by pure adrenaline.

Our History

FUTA Rafting was born as a family business run by local residents of the Futaleufú region, deeply connected to the river and its surroundings. What started as a small community initiative grew into a trusted adventure company, sharing the magic of the Futaleufú River with visitors from all over the world.
